Course Overview

IoT (Internet of Things) connects everyday devices to the internet, allowing them to collect, share, and analyze data. It creates smart systems that enhance efficiency, automation, and decision-making across industries such as healthcare, agriculture, homes, and manufacturing through sensors, cloud computing, and intelligent connectivity.

Curriculum

  • Basics of IoT architecture and components.
  • Working with sensors, actuators, and microcontrollers.
  • Understanding IoT communication protocols (Wi-Fi, MQTT, Bluetooth, etc.).
  • Data collection, storage, and analysis through cloud platforms.
  • Security and privacy in IoT systems.
  • Real-world IoT applications in smart homes, cities, and industries.
